Manicures Types & Prices

Traditional Manicure/Regular Polish .............................. 35 min / $30

Main Focus: Hygiene, Nail Care.

Regular Polish Removal (if any), cuticle remover & push back, cuticle oil, trimming, filing, buffing, hands exfoliating& moisturizing finished with regular polish if any. Time & cost-effective procedure that makes hands look well-groomed, slows down the growth of extra cuticles, softens hands skin.

Spa Manicure/Regular Polish ........................................... 45 min / $40

Main Focus: Hygiene, Nail Care, Relaxation, Rejuvenating

Regular Polish Removal (if any), hands soak, cuticle work, cuticle oil, trimming, filing, buffing, exfoliation, massage, moisturizing finished with regular polish if any. A relaxing & rejuvenating procedure that makes hands look well-groomed, slows down the growth of extra cuticles. Improves blood circulation and hands skin tone.

Dip Powder Manicure ................................................. 1 hr 20 min / $45

Main Focus: Hygiene, Nail Care, No UV Curing

Old Material Removal (if any) with E-File. Cuticle work, trimming, filing, buffing, dip powder. More durable than gel polish, lasts up to 3 weeks.

Russian Manicure/Regular Polish Promo .......... 1 hr 30 min / $40

Main Focus: Hygiene, Advance Nail Care

Using various E-file extensions, extra cuticle removal is combined with detailed work on nail sidewalls & nail plates followed by polishing of the cuticles, oil application, and finished with regular polish if any. This popular in Europe procedure results in a long-lasting, up to 4 weeks, beautiful manicure that slows down the growth of extra cuticles, gently and efficiently eliminates rough skin around nail plates, and corrects their shape. Fits anyone who appreciates immaculate manicure.

Russian Manicure/Gel Polish Promo ................................... 2hr / $50

Main Focus: Hygiene, Advance Nail Care, Advanced Color Application & Nail Plate Leveling

Using various E-file extensions, extra cuticle removal is combined with detailed work on nail sidewalls & nail plates followed by polishing of the cuticles, oil application, and finished with Gel polish. This popular in Europe procedure results in a long-lasting up to 4 weeks, beautiful manicure that slows down the growth of extra cuticles, gently and efficiently eliminates rough skin around nail plates, levels the plates, and corrects their shape. Fits anyone who appreciates immaculate manicure and flawless beauty of gel color application.

Kid's Manicure (Age Under 12)/ Regular Polish ......... 25 min / $20

Main Focus: Hygiene, Nail Care

Regular Polish Removal (if any), cuticle push back, cuticle oil, trimming, filing, buffing, hands moisturizing finished with regular color application & free basic design of our choice on two fingers. A gentle procedure that makes child's hands look well-groomed and fun.
